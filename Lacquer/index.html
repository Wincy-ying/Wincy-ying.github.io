漆情画艺

非遗艺术数字化开发与扶贫项目

Introduction

| 简介

本项目致力于漆艺艺术的数字化传承和融合媒体推广,通过媒体传播技术挖掘和传播漆艺艺术,并对其进行符合当下市场消费需求的创意开发,大力发挥 非遗 的核心作用,发展区域经济,带动就业,实现海上丝绸之路文化传播


The project is committed to digital inheritance and integrated media promotion of lacquer art, mining and spreading lacquer art through media communication technology, and conducting creative development in line with current market consumption demand, vigorously playing the core role of intangible cultural heritage, developing regional economy, driving employment, and realizing the cultural dissemination of the Maritime Silk Road.
